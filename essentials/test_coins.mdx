Learn how to get test coins for testing app in test networks
<Warning> 
Remember

 Testnet coins are a special type of cryptocurrency used for testing purposes. 
 
 They can be used to test out new features and functionality without putting real money at risk. 
 
 They can also be used to learn how the blockchain works without the risk of losing real money.
</Warning>

## Ethereum Sepolia Faucet

Use [Ethereum Sepolia Faucet](https://cloud.google.com/application/web3/faucet/ethereum/sepolia) to get Sepolia Ethereum

Just put your wallet addres into field

## Circle faucet

Use [Circle Faucet](https://faucet.circle.com/) to get USDC testnet coins for Arbitrim Seploia, Ethereum Sepolia, Base Sepolia

Just put your wallet addres into field and select network

## Bridges

Use [Arbitrium bridge](https://portal.arbitrum.io/bridge?destinationChain=arbitrum-sepolia&sanitized=true&sourceChain=sepolia) to send ETH SEPOLIA token into your Arbitrium Sepolia balance

Also you can use [Superbridge](https://superbridge.app/base-sepolia) to send ETH SEPOLIA token into Base Sepolia balance

<Info> 
Important Notes

You should have some balance in base coin (ETH for Sepolia Test Networks) to pay gas (network fee). In L2 networks like Arbitrium, Base gas is very low
</Info>
