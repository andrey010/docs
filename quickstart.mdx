## Get started in three steps

Get your web app accept crypto payments.


<Steps>
<Step title="Create account">
  Sign up using our [Web app](https://app.paylora.org/login).
    
  Or use our [Telegram bot](https://t.me/paylora_bot)
</Step>
<Step title="Prepare crypto wallet">
 Configure your crypto wallet [see crypto wallet guide](essentials/crypto_wallet) and add request whitelist
</Step>
<Step title="Create first invoice">
 Create invoice in our web app or add button to your telegram chanel using our telgram bot and get payment.
</Step>
</Steps>
